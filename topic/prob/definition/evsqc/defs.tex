\def\evsqcdef#1{%
for \ctx\ $\SSPT{\SPT}{(\XV,\X),(\YV,\Y)}$, and all $\SQQ \in \SQ$, $\fnnot{f}{\X \times \Y}{\R}$ and $\pin\YY\Y$,
  $$\ESQC{\SQQ}{f}{\YV = \YY} = \lim_{\TR \rightarrow \infty}
  \left(
  \frac
  {\sum_{\TR' = 1}^{\TR}\sum_{\pin{\XX}{\X}}\SQI{\SQQ}{\TR'}{\XV = \XX, \YV = \YY}\times f(\XX, \YY)}
  {\sum_{\TR' = 1}^{\TR}\SQIM{\SQQ}{\TR'}{\YV = \YY}}
  \right)$$
  if such a limit exists, and undefined otherwise. 
  
  This is verbally referred to as the ``\expvsqc'' of $f$ under $\SQQ$#1%
}
